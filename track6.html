Description

This track begins with a very straightforward drum pattern laid neatly on top of some piano chords, and right after we hear a distant "hello" an extremely distorted vocal lead takes over. This lead sounds nearly inhuman, so despite it likely being a sample from Matt Van, it is impossible to be certain.

The track continues with the pattern of interlude/vocal melody, adding small segments to the vocals each time. After a small rise, the song ends with some short piano notes, and repetition of the "hello" sample.
