Track One is the title track of the album, Lost Lost Lost. It begins with repeating piano arpeggios, intense synths, and a bold lead vocal from the uncredited Matt Van.

Van sings the main line of the song "girl you've got me feeling so lost" which launches the track into an abyss of synths, rumbling bass sounds, and heavy claps.

Holly Drummond also lent vocals to this track, creating a beautiful duet between any two people or symbols the listener could imagine.
