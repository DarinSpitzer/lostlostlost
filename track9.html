Description

Friendly Ghosts fades from static to what feels like nothing. There are no drums, no synths, just static and piano for the first 40 seconds, until a sound similar to the horn lead in To Belong comes in.

This song seems like an interlude, but at over three minutes long (and only three seconds shorter than Im Alright) it cannot really be classified as one. Minimalist drums enter at 1:36, accompanying a pulsating bassline.

This track is a spectacular way to prepare the listener for the grand finale that is track ten, Let the Sky Fall Down.
