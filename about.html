Brendan Galdo, professionally known as Mr. Fijiwiji is an electronic music producer that has been active since his debut single "Insomia" in 2011 at the age of 15.

Since then, he has released numerous singles on the Canadian record label Monstercat. Most of his music falls into the chillout, future, and dubstep genres.

In 2014, he released his EP titled "Growing Up" at the time he was graduating high school and beginning college.

In 2016, He released a longer project, titled "Dogma" which was was five tracks long.

In the following year, 2017, Galdo collaborated with Matt Van twice, on the tracks "Tomorrow" with Direct, a fellow producer and friend, and "Andromeda" with Exist Strategy.
