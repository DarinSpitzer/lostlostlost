Description

Identical to every track, this song fades in with static, but slowly leads into this low, distant vocal. Galdo's signature synth takes over after a few measures, and a clearer female vocal is also added to the mix.

These three sounds balance on top of a slow but punchy beat for the remainder of the track. In terms of emotional expression without words, this track is definately a standout from this album.
